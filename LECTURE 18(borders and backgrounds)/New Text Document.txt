BORDERS AND BACKGROUNDS:-

 background-color: blueviolet;
            height: 100px;
            width: 300px;
            border-width: 4px;
            border-style:solid;
            border-color: brown;

->First we choose bg color
->Second we give height to the background as well widht (jtni ham dena chahy)
->Third hamne border width di h jsiki madad sa ham border ki patti laga skte hein (more than 4px agar pati moti chiye) basically pati ko he border bolty hein
->Fourth hamne border style solid rkha h(koi b rkh skty hein)
->Last hamne border ko color dia h

	    border-width: 4px;
            border-style:solid;
            border-color: brown;

If we want these three to be in a single line we use , border: 2px solid green; (in a single line)

If we want border corners to be curvy we use:-

border-radius: property in pixels(px)

If we want only give border to one side we use border-top: 4px solid green , border-bottom: , border-left: , border-right: etc

like this ham radius b apni mrzi se de skte hein , border-top-left-radius:10px , border-top-right-radius: , border-bottom-right-radius: , bottom-bottom-left-radius:etc
